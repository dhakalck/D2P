What is Data to Policy?

The Data to Policy (D2P) program creates sustainable capacity for government health professionals to develop evidence-informed policy recommendations. Co-led by the U.S. CDC and Vital Strategies as part of Bloomberg Philanthropies' Data for Health Initiative.

The D2P Process

1

Unit 1: Problem & Policy Analysis

2 weeks - Frame problems, conduct root cause analysis, identify policy options

2

Unit 2: Economic Evaluation

1 week - Develop decision trees, cost analyses, and cost-effectiveness evaluations

3

Mentorship Period

8-10 weeks - Intensive guidance and policy brief development

4

Unit 3: Advocacy

1 week - Finalize briefs, develop presentations, advocacy strategies

5

Policy Forum

Present to government officials and work toward implementation
